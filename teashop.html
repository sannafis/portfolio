Tea Shop (Asp.Net MVC)

Design

Premise: This is an Inventory/Order/Sales management application for a (fictional) tea shop selling tea products and fresh tea to local customers. I want to the application to match the relaxing feel of the shop for the staff.
These are mockup designs I've made for just some of the pages on the site.

Data Model

The data model is still a work in progress, so this is not the final version. I'm trying to design a data model that is flexible and considers the needs for a shop that has both menu items and shelf products. This aspect also ties back into the design of the site.
